FAQs

Curious about the most asked questions about SatisFacts? Check out our Frequently Asked Questions to get quick answers, tips, and helpful guidance. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, reach out to our support team for assistance.

How long does it take to answer?

The survey is designed to be short and simple. On average, it will only take you 2–3 minutes to complete. Most questions are multiple-choice or rating scales, so you can quickly share your feedback without any hassle.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All responses are encrypted and stored securely in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012. No personal information is required unless you choose to provide it.

What will my feedback be used for?

Your feedback is used to evaluate and improve government services. Results are analyzed under ARTA standards to make processes more efficient and citizen-friendly.

Can I answer the survey offline?

Yes. You can complete the survey even without internet. Your answers are saved locally and will sync automatically once you reconnect online.
